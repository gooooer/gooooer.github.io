In this important new book, David Deutsch, an award-winning pioneer in the field of quantum computation, argues that <I>explanations</I> have a fundamental place in the universe. They have unlimited scope and power to cause change, and the quest to improve them is the basic regulating principle not only of science but of all successful human endeavor.
