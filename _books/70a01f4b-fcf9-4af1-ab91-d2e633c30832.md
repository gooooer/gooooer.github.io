Hacker culture can be esoteric, but this entertaining reference is here to help. This fun reference introduces you to key people and companies, fundamental ideas, and milestone films, games, and magazines in the annals of hacking. From phreaking to deepfakes, and from APT to zombie malware, grasping the terminology is crucial to understanding hacker culture and history. This book from Kim Crawley is written in a conversational style and organized from A to Z. Read it like a coffee table book or a desk reference.
