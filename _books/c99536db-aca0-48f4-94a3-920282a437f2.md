Using a well-conceived incident response plan in the aftermath of an online security breach enables your team to identify attackers and learn how they operate. But only when you approach incident response with a cyber threat intelligence mindset will you truly understand the value of that information. With this updated edition, you'll learn the fundamentals of intelligence analysis including the best ways to incorporate these techniques into your incident response process.
